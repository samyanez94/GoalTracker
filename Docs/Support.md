# Moku Support

Need help with Moku?

Moku is a personal goal and habit tracker for creating goals, tracking measurable progress, setting reminders, organizing goals with tags, and reviewing progress history.

## Common Questions

### Does Moku require an account?

No. Moku does not require an account.

### Where is my data stored?

Moku stores your goal data on your device. If iCloud is enabled on your device and available for Moku, your goal data may sync using your private iCloud account.

### How do reminders work?

If you enable reminders, Moku schedules notifications for your target dates or recurring goals. You can manage notification permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

### How do I delete my data?

You can delete goals and progress history from within Moku. You can also delete the app or manage associated iCloud data through Apple's device and iCloud settings.

### How can I report a bug or request a feature?

Email [samuelyanez94@gmail.com](mailto:samuelyanez94@gmail.com) with a short description of the issue or idea. If you are reporting a bug, please include your device model, iOS version, and the steps that led to the problem.
