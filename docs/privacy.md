# Moku Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 6, 2026

Moku is a personal goal and habit tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains what information Moku handles and how it is used.

## Information You Add to Moku

Moku lets you create and manage goals, habits, measurable progress, tags, target dates, recurrence settings, reminders, and progress history.

This information is created by you and stored for the purpose of showing and managing your goals in the app.

## Local Storage and iCloud Sync

Moku stores your goal data on your device.

If iCloud is enabled on your device and available for Moku, your goal data may sync using Apple's iCloud service so it can stay up to date across your devices. This sync uses your private iCloud account. The developer of Moku does not operate a separate server for your goal data and does not access your private iCloud data.

Apple's handling of iCloud data is governed by Apple's privacy practices.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, Moku may schedule local notifications for your goals or recurring habits. Notifications are used only to remind you about goals you create.

You can manage notification permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

## Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

Moku does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Moku does not sell your data.

Moku does not share your goal data with advertisers or data brokers.

## Data Sharing

Moku does not share your goal data with the developer or third parties.

Your data may be processed by Apple services, such as iCloud, if you choose to use those services on your device.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your goal data remains in Moku until you delete it, delete the app, or remove associated iCloud data through Apple's device and iCloud settings.

You can delete goals and progress history from within the app.

## Children's Privacy

Moku is not directed to children under 13. Moku does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If the policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the support contact listed on Moku's App Store product page.
